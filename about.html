Who We Are

Horizon Realty is a full-service real estate agency helping buyers and sellers with single family homes, condos, townhomes, luxury estates, commercial property, land, rentals, and waterfront listings.

What Our Platform Offers

  • 20+ listing categories with multiple options per property
  • Mortgage Calculator for payment estimates
  • Market Stats with live currency conversion
  • Buying Guide and Help Wiki
  • Contact form for viewings and inquiries
  • Fully responsive modern design

Technology & Updates

The website uses three seasonal themes (Classic, Spring, Christmas) that switch automatically. We integrate live exchange rates and local weather data for accurate budgeting and planning.

Listings can be updated easily through the listings.json file without modifying HTML pages.
